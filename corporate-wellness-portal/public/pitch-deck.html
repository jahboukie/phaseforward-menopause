💔 Where It All Began: A Husband's Desperate Search for Understanding

"What started as a bootstrap attempt to save my marriage became the foundation for transforming healthcare for billions."

🌙 The 3 AM Revelation

It was another sleepless night. My wife was sobbing in the bathroom, overwhelmed by perimenopause symptoms that neither of us understood. I felt completely helpless, watching the woman I love struggle through mood swings and physical changes that seemed to come from nowhere.

I was failing as a husband.

Every time I tried to help, I made it worse. Every conversation felt like walking through a minefield. I was taking her outbursts personally, creating more pain in moments when she needed support most.

💡 The Desperate Solution

That night, I made a decision that would change everything. If I couldn't understand what my wife was going through, maybe AI could teach me. I decided to build an app - not for her, but for me - to help me become the partner she deserved during this impossible time.

I wanted Claude AI to guide me through the emotional minefield of menopause so I could stop failing the woman I love.

🔥 The Unexpected Discovery

As Claude Code and I started building what would become the SupportPartner app, something extraordinary happened. The more we developed, the more I realized the massive gap in healthcare support for women and their families.

It wasn't just my wife. It was millions of women, suffering in silence, with partners who felt just as lost and helpless as I did.

⚡ The Moment Everything Changed

What began as one desperate husband's attempt to save his marriage evolved into something much bigger:

  • MenoWellness - Because every woman deserves comprehensive support
  • SupportPartner - Because love requires understanding
  • Six more applications - Covering the complete human health journey
  • Dr. Alex AI - Providing medical-grade clinical intelligence
  • SentimentAsAService - Turning our pain into insights that help millions

💰 The Bootstrap Reality Check

As our vision expanded from one app to a complete healthcare ecosystem, I realized something crucial: The world needs this too badly to bootstrap it slowly.

Every day we delay is another day millions of women suffer without support. Another day partners feel helpless. Another day healthcare providers lack the tools to provide truly compassionate, proactive care.

This mission is bigger than my personal resources. It requires the scale and speed that only strategic investment can provide.
